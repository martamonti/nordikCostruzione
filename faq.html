Questions?

  • What is RedPen?

    RedPen is a multi-platform digital service specifically designed for distance learning, especially for primary school. RedPen offers all the necessary tools to the teaching staff and pupils for to carry out all aspects of teaching in the best possible way.

  • How it works?

    RedPen works in complete synchronization with the school of reference. It takes the data directly from the school's database and integrates it 360 degrees with the digital system. The service allows children to do their homework with direct on platform tools.

  • Who uses RedPen?

    RedPen is used by teaching staff and primary school pupils.

  • How can I edit a template?

    In the "assign task" section you can go to edit all the templates through specific tools and through the keyboard of your device.
